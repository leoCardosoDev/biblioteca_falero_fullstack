# User Story (STORY_03)

## Title
Library Unit Management

## Persona
Administrator

## Story
As an **Administrator**,
I want **to manage Physical Units (Libraries/Schools)**,
So that **inventory and loans can be tracked by location.**

## Business Value
- Enables multi-branch library operations.
- Critical for inventory tracking (where is the book?).
- Defines origin points for loans.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **Physical Presence**: Every Unit MUST have a complete, valid address.
- [ ] **Contact Info**: Unit must have a phone number.
- [ ] **Geography Validation**: Unit address must validate against Geography Kernel.

## Out of Scope
- Internal floor mapping of the unit.
- Hours of operation management (Phase 2).

## Assumptions
- The system supports multiple units from day one.

## Open Questions
- None.
