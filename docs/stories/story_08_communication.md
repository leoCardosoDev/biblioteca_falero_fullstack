# User Story (STORY_08)

## Title
Communication & Support

## Persona
Visitor (Public)

## Story
As a **Visitor**,
I want **to send contact messages to the library administration**,
So that **I can ask questions, suggest books, or report issues.**

## Business Value
- Opens a channel for feedback and support.
- Does not require a registered account (barriers to entry removed).

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **Public Access**: Form is accessible without login.
- [ ] **Data Capture**: Name, Email, Message, Helpers.
- [ ] **Admin Reply**: Admins can view and reply to messages.
- [ ] **Timestamps**: Track Received At and Replied At.

## Out of Scope
- Real-time chat.
- Ticket system integration (Zendesk etc).

## Assumptions
- Simple email notification might be triggered (out of band).

## Open Questions
- Spam protection? (Recaptcha - Phase 2).
